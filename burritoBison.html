Burrito Bison: Launcha Libre

In Every Burrito Bison game, Burrito Bison can achieve a special technique called the Slam. Slam activates automatically when Bison falls for a certain period of time and reaches the falling speed threshold before hitting the ground typically, the strength of which is represented by vertical wind sheer. When Burritio Bison reaches the ground with at least one level of Slam, he will create a small explosion that also negates any loss of speed upon hitting the ground similar to the Rocket Slam. In Burrito Bison: Launcha Libre, The slam returns as Burrito Bison's main ability, which the other Launchadores cannot use likely due to their lack of weight compared to him.
